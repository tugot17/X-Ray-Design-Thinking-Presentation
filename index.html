Problem

Cardiothoracic ratio

CTR > $\frac{1}{2}$ $\implies$ Cardiomegaly

Perspectives

Radiologist: "I want to know which abnormalities are on the image and where they are"

Cardiologist: "I want to know whether the CTR is higher or smaller than 0.5"

Medical Imaging Researcher: "I want to know what is the current sota for the problem of abnormality detection in chest x-rays"

Author: I want to write research paper, which will help other researchers in this field

Author: I want my application to be easy to use and available

Author: I want to increase my competence in the field of Data Science.
